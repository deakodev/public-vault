### Clinical Decision Support and Attentional Capture
*Research · 2026*

My dissertation. Investigates how clinicians interact with decision support tools during time-critical workflows — specifically the mismatch between what a system surfaces and what a provider actually attends to. The work draws on joint cognitive systems theory, signal detection theory, and attentional resource models.

### Medical Education Platform
*Project · 2025*

A platform built to fix how medical students study — structured around active recall, spaced repetition, and cross-linking clinical content across disciplines. Built with the constraint that it has to be fast enough to use between patients on a clinical rotation.

[Read more →](/projects/medstudforty)

### Tangible Neuroanatomy
*Project · 2025*

3D-printed brain region models combined with an AR overlay that surfaces labels, functions, and clinical correlates. An experiment in whether physical manipulation changes how medical students encode spatial neuroanatomy — it does.
