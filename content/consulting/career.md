I've navigated the path from pre-med to medical school to doctoral research. I know what committees are looking for and how to help you present your work honestly and compellingly. I work with applicants at every stage -- from building a competitive profile early on to refining the final draft of a personal statement.

***Disclaimer**: I am not affiliated with any medical or graduate school admissions committee, and I cannot guarantee outcomes. Advice reflects my own experience and perspective.*

---
## What I can help with

- **Career path conversations** -- navigating the intersection of medicine, research, and industry
- **Application strategy** -- school selection, timeline planning, and how to present a non-traditional or research-heavy profile
- **Resume/CV development** -- structure, framing, and presentation for medical and graduate school applications
- **Personal statement review** -- drafting, revision, and honest critical feedback
- **Interview preparation** -- MMI, traditional, and panel formats for medical and PhD programs

Rates vary by scope and engagement type.
