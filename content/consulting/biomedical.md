I hold a DO degree and am currently a PhD student in Human-Centered Computing at Georgia Tech. My background spans clinical medicine, biomedical science, and computing, with a focus on health technology design, point-of-care sensing, and clinical workflow research. If your project touches those areas and you need someone who can hold both the technical and clinical frame at the same time, I may be able to help.

_Disclaimer: I do not hold an active medical license in any state. Nothing offered here constitutes medical advice, clinical diagnosis, or treatment recommendation. All engagements are advisory and educational in nature._

**What I can help with**

- Health technology design and evaluation: wearables, point-of-care devices, remote monitoring systems, and clinical decision support tools
- Human-centered design for clinical contexts: clinician workflow analysis, user research in healthcare settings, and translating clinical need into design requirements
- Research design and methodology: study design, outcome selection, and framing for projects at the biomedical-computing intersection
- Technical writing and grant narrative: helping teams without clinical backgrounds write credibly about health domains
- Literature synthesis: rapid domain reviews for teams entering biomedical or clinical spaces

Rates vary by scope and engagement type.
